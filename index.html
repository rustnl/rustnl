Stichting Rust Nederland

Stichting Rust Nederland is a non-profit with the goal to promote the implementation of the Rust programming language in the Netherlands and Europe.

We organize meetups and events in The Netherlands to bring the Rust community together.

Find us on:

Meetup: meetup.com/rust-nederland
GitHub: github.com/rustnl
YouTube: youtube.com/@rustnederlandrustnl
